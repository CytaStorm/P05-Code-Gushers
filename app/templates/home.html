Welcome to CodeGushers's

Duckies Crush

Match 3 Game



How To Play

Match three duckies together of the same color! Each duck clear scores 10 points.


Power-Ups and Combos

Striped Ducks

In order to obtain a striped duck, the player must clear 4 ducks in a row or column. The orientation the player clears the ducks (horizontal or vertical) determines the direction the striped ducks will clear (horizontal or vertical).


Bomb Ducks




In order to obtain a bomb duck, the player must connect ducks in a "L" or "T" shape. Clears a radius of 2 ducks in every direction.


Rainbow Ducks

In order to obtain a rainbow duck, the player must clear 5 ducks in a row or column. The special duck clears all ducks of the same color.


Game Modes


Coin Mode

Players can play the game for as long as they want, as long as they have the coins to make a move. Each move costs 1 coin. The player earns a coin for each 100 points the player scores.


Clear Mode

Players are given a board of ducklings that they must clear. All the same combos and powerups apply.
